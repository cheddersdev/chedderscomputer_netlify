Our Story

Born in Lagos Island.
Built on Trust.

Chedders Computer started with a simple belief: Nigerians deserve access to 100% genuine, brand-new technology without the uncertainty of grey-market deals or questionable refurbishments.

Operating from BNB Mall in the heart of Golf Estate, Lagos Island, we've spent years building relationships with authorised suppliers and earning the trust of thousands of satisfied customers across Nigeria.

We sell one thing — brand new laptops — and we do it better than anyone else. Every unit is factory-sealed, carries the full manufacturer's warranty, and is sourced from legitimate channels.
